Ella is a Siberian Husky with all the energy of one. She needs constant runs, walks, and playtime. Ella will let you know if she is unhappy whether that be by barking, running in circles, or just destroying everything in sight. She is a great dog, but quite the handful.

About

Playtime is by far Ella's favorite. She will run around the dog park for over an hour or play fetch and chase with every toy she has. Ella loves other dogs, random people, and all her toys. Whether it is fetch, chase, or tug Ella will be happy no matter the playtime.

Ella

Ella Is A Good Girl

Ella is well on her way to being a great girl, but a lot of work must be done.
