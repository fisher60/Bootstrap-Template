Ella

Ella is a typical Siberian Husky. She has incredible amounts of energy and demands constant mental and physical exercise. While we love her for this, she can also be quite the handful. She loves to keep herself and everyone in her immediate viscinity busy with her. An average day consists of around a one hour walk that is followed by bursts of playtime all throughout the day. To keep Ella entertained and content it is also necessary to throw in training periodically between playtime.

Not only does Ella live her everyday life at full throttle, but also her adventures. She gets to experience many new places--places many dogs would never normally see. From the coast to the mountains, sea-level to Flight Level 350, Ella has seen quite a lot. Even so, Ella's favorite locations are simply the dog-beach and the dog-park. She loves all things social.
